**Exit Criteria**

*Testing will be considered complete when the following conditions are met*:

* All planned Test Cases have been executed.

* All high-severity and critical defects found have been reported and documented.

* All defects identified during testing are either fixed, deferred, or accepted as known limitations.

* Test Summary Report is prepared and delivered.

* No blocking issues remain in critical business workflows (orders, payments, product management, reporting).
