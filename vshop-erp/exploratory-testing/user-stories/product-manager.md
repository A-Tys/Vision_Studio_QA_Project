# Product Manager User Stories
<br>
<br>

## Product Catalog Management
- As a Product Manager, I want to add, edit, or remove products in the catalog so that the product list is always up-to-date.  
- As a Product Manager, I want to categorize products (by type, manufacturer, etc.) so that the catalog is easy to navigate.  
- As a Product Manager, I want to upload product images and descriptions so that customers have accurate information.  
<br>
<br>

## Pricing
- As a Product Manager, I want to update product prices in bulk (price lists, price updates) so that the system reflects market changes efficiently.  
- As a Product Manager, I want to check prices against marketplace benchmarks (e.g., Skroutz) so that our pricing remains competitive.  
- As a Product Manager, I want to export price lists to Excel so that I can share them with management or suppliers.
<br>
<br>

## Supplier & Manufacturer Coordination
- As a Product Manager, I want to manage manufacturer and supplier information so that purchasing and communication are streamlined.  
- As a Product Manager, I want to receive updates about new supplier catalogs so that the system always reflects available products.  
<br>
<br>

## Product Performance
- As a Product Manager, I want to review product sales statistics so that I can identify bestsellers and underperforming items.  
- As a Product Manager, I want to track product returns and defects so that I can identify quality issues and inform suppliers.
